Feedback & Suggestions

Help us make CityuShop better for every student on campus.

Your voice matters

What kind of feedback can you share?

You can send us any thoughts about using CityuShop. We welcome both positive feedback and constructive suggestions, for example:

  • Ideas to improve the buying or selling experience
  • Suggestions for new product categories or features
  • Questions about security, privacy, or transaction safety
  • Layout, interface, or usability improvements you would like to see
Please describe your suggestion as clearly as possible (including screenshots or links if needed) so that our team can understand and evaluate it more quickly.

How to submit suggestions?

Currently we support the following ways to send your suggestions:

  • Send an email to: cityushop-support@example.com
  • Contact the platform administrator through the campus communication tools
  • Leave feedback when participating in campus events related to CityuShop

We review feedback regularly. If your suggestion involves important functions or bugs, we will prioritize processing and, when appropriate, announce updates on the homepage.
